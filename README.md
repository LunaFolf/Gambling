# Chamberlocked
Breathe... slowly... Don't panic... your life might just depend on it...

## About

Chamberlocked is a psychological-horror strategy, based on the lethal game of chance Russian Roulette. After forgetting your wallet at a casino and racking up a sizeable debt, management has decided they have a way you can pay them back afterall...

## How to Play

Uses controller for input (Built for Playstation 5 controller, developed using an Xbox One Controller).

DPAD Left/Right to navigate menu options, X (A) to select options.

Right Trigger to shoot Rival, Left Trigger to shoot self...

Listen the to bullets when the Rival reloads, you might just notice a difference...

## Known Bugs

- Audio when bullets are reloaded is sometimes backwards/reverse order.
- Returning the Main Menu after completing game, and attempting to start a new game can sometimes soft-lock the game on a black screen.
- Dialogue speech soundeffects can sometimes "clip".
